MY RECOMMENDATION
A — Realistic Cosmic Objects. The whole point of the Atlas is the cosmic metaphor. Galaxies should look like galaxies — spiral particle systems with glow halos. Stars should pulse and have corona effects. Planets should orbit. This is what makes CopyClip's Atlas unforgettable and what competitors can't replicate. Abstract shapes would work but would feel like every other graph tool.
