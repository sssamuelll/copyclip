Edge Visual Style

How should the connections between modules render in 3D space?

A

Straight Lines (Obsidian-faithful)

Simple straight lines between connected nodes. Clean, minimal, exactly like Obsidian's graph. Opacity/thickness based on connection strength.

Thin, semi-transparent cyan lines
B

Glowing Filaments (cosmic)

Lines with additive blending glow, like energy streams between stars. Brighter where connections are stronger. Subtle animated pulse along the line.

Glowing lines with energy feel
C

Minimal by Default, Glow on Focus

Very faint lines at rest (almost invisible). When you hover/select a node, its edges light up with a glow. Keeps the view clean when exploring, reveals structure on demand.

at rest
on hover/select
MY RECOMMENDATION
C — Minimal by default, glow on focus. With up to 800 edges, always-visible lines would turn the view into a hairball. Obsidian has the same problem at scale and solves it with opacity fade. Option C goes further: edges are nearly invisible until you interact, then they reveal the structure around the focused node. Best of both worlds.
