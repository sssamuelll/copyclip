Always Visible (Obsidian-style)

Every node shows its label at all times. Labels face the camera (billboarding). Opacity fades with distance so far-away labels don't clutter.

MY RECOMMENDATION
C — Hybrid. The top 8-10 most-connected modules act as landmarks you can always orient by. Smaller nodes reveal their names on hover. This gives you Obsidian's readability for the important nodes without the clutter of labeling every single module. The landmark labels also serve as natural anchor points when navigating the 3D space.
